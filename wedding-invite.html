Location:

Campbell House Museum

Built in 1822, Campbell House is the oldest surviving building from the Town of York and an outstanding example of Georgian architecture. Moved to its current location in 1972, Campbell House was home to Chief Justice William Campbell.

Address: 160 Queen St. West,Toronto, ON M5H 3H3

It is located at the northwest corner of the intersection of Queen Street & University Ave, slightly west to the City Hall.
